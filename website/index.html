FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is ManifestAI: Gratitude Journal?

ManifestAI is an iOS app that combines daily gratitude journaling with AI-powered insights, Daily Numerology, Tesla's 369 manifestation method, and Vision Boards — designed to fit into just 5 minutes of your day.

How does the 3-day free trial work?

You get full access to every feature — journaling, numerology, the 369 method, and vision boards — free for 3 days. After the trial, your subscription continues at $24.99 per year (or $3.99 per week) unless you cancel before it ends.

What is the Tesla 369 manifestation method?

It's a manifestation technique inspired by Nikola Tesla's belief that the numbers 3, 6, and 9 hold the key to the universe. In the app, you write your affirmation 3 times in the morning, 6 times in the afternoon, and 9 times at night to reinforce your intention.

How is my Daily Numerology calculated?

ManifestAI calculates your personal day number from your birth date and today's date, then delivers a short daily insight so you know what energy to lean into before you start journaling.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings, App Store subscription page — no need to contact us.

Is my journal private?

Yes. Your gratitude entries, vision boards, and reflections are personal to you — ManifestAI does not share your journal content with other users or advertisers.
