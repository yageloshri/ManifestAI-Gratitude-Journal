369 Method Examples: What to Write (25+ Affirmations)

6 min read · Manifest Guides

The hardest part of the 369 method isn't the writing — it's knowing what to write. Here's the formula that makes an affirmation work, followed by 25+ examples you can copy for love, money, career, health, and self-worth.

The anatomy of a 369 affirmation

Every strong 369 sentence has four parts:

  1. Gratitude opener — "I am so happy and grateful now that…" puts you in receiving mode.
  2. Present tense — the desire is already yours. No "want," "will," "hope," or "someday."
  3. Specific detail — a number, a name, a scene your mind can render.
  4. Emotion tag — "…and I feel free / safe / radiant." Feeling is the amplifier.
The formula

"I am so happy and grateful now that [specific desire, present tense], and I feel [emotion]."

Keep it short enough to write in about 15 seconds — you'll write it 18 times a day for 33 days. If you're new to the schedule itself, start with how to do the 369 method.

Love & relationship examples

  • "I am so grateful to be deeply loved by a partner who chooses me every day, and I feel safe and cherished."
  • "Thank you for the healthy, playful relationship I'm in — we communicate with ease and I feel at home."
  • "I am so happy that love finds me effortlessly; I attract people who match my energy."
  • "My relationship with [name] grows warmer and more honest every day, and I feel connected."
  • "I am grateful that I love myself first, and the love around me mirrors it."

Money & abundance examples

  • "I am so happy and grateful that money flows to me from expected and unexpected sources, and I feel secure."
  • "Thank you for the $2,000 of extra income I receive this month with ease."
  • "I am debt-free and in control of my finances, and I feel light and proud."
  • "My savings grow every single week, and abundance feels normal to me."
  • "I am grateful that I am paid generously for work I genuinely enjoy."

Career & success examples

  • "I am so grateful for my new role at [company] — meaningful work, a kind team, and a salary that lets me breathe."
  • "Thank you for the clients who love my work and recommend me without being asked."
  • "My business earns [amount] a month serving people I care about, and I feel unstoppable."
  • "I walk into every interview calm and magnetic; the right opportunity recognizes me."
  • "I am grateful that my creative work reaches the people who need it."

Health & energy examples

  • "I am so grateful for my strong, energized body that carries me through the day with ease."
  • "Thank you for my calm mind — I sleep deeply and wake up rested."
  • "Every day my body grows healthier and lighter, and I feel alive."
  • "I treat my body with kindness, and it responds with strength."

Self-worth & peace examples

  • "I am so grateful that I trust myself completely, and I feel grounded."
  • "Thank you for the quiet confidence I carry into every room."
  • "I release what I can't control, and peace is my default setting."
  • "I am becoming the person I used to dream about, and I feel proud."
  • "I am worthy of everything I'm asking for — as I am, today."

How to personalize any example

Copying a line verbatim is a fine day one. By day three, make it yours:

  • Swap in your specifics — real names, real numbers, your city, your craft.
  • Say it aloud once. If your body tenses with disbelief, soften the claim one notch ("more and more each day…") until it feels true-adjacent, then grow it.
  • Keep one sentence per cycle. Pick your favorite and commit for 33 days — variety is for the next cycle.

Can I manifest more than one thing at once?

Run one affirmation per 33-day cycle. Splitting your 18 daily repetitions across three desires gives each one a third of the focus — and focus is the entire mechanism. If two desires feel equally urgent, ask which one, fulfilled, would make the other easier, and start there. The next cycle begins in five weeks; the queue moves faster than you think.
