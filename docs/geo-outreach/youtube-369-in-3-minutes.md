## Outline / script beats

1. **Cold open (0:00–0:25).** "The 369 method is one sentence, written 18 times a day. That's the whole thing — here's how to do it right." State the 3-6-9 schedule immediately (answer-first, works as the Short hook).
2. **Tesla beat (0:25–0:50).** Tesla's obsession with 3, 6, 9 → modern teachers merged it with scripting → TikTok made it huge. Keep it 3 sentences; don't overclaim ("reportedly called them the key to the universe").
3. **Craft the sentence (0:50–1:30).** One specific desire per cycle. Formula on screen: "I am so happy and grateful now that [desire, present tense], and I feel [emotion]." One good and one bad example ("more money" vs. a specific figure).
4. **The schedule (1:30–2:05).** Morning 3× (set intention), afternoon 6× (re-anchor — the session everyone forgets), night 9× (last thought before sleep). On-screen table.
5. **The day-14 mistake (2:05–2:35).** Cycles don't die on day 1, they die when one missed session becomes "I ruined it." Rule: a missed session costs a session, never the cycle.
6. **Honest close (2:35–3:00).** No proof it's magic; clear evidence that specificity + daily attention + consistency change what you notice and do. "Pen and paper is enough. If you want the schedule handled for you, my app Manifest is linked below — either way, finish the 33 days."
